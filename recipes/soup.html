Egusi_Soup

Description

Right here is a step by by process to prepare one of Nigerias most delicious soups

Ingredients

  • Egusi
  • Crayfish
  • Assorted meat
  • 2 Stock fish
  • 2 Dry fish
  • Scotch bonnet
  • 1 large onion bulb
  • Black pepper
  • 2 Calabar nutmeg
  • 4 Seasoning cubes
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • Palm oil
  • Beef seasoning
  • Dry ginger or fresh ginger
  • 3 garlic clove
  • 2 cups water
Steps
  1. Assemble your ingredients.
  2. Soak the stock fish in hot water for 20min
  3. Wash your meats and place in pot and add blended scotch bonnet mixture
  4. Add the stock fish and coarsely grounded black pepper, ginger and nutmeg
  5. Add Salt
  6. Beef seasoning
  7. Seasoning cubes
  8. Slice onions
  9. Add water enough to cover the meat. I add 2 cups because i want my meat to be soft especially because it has cow skin (ganda)
  10. Ground your crashfish and mix with the egusi
  11. Mix till combinedi
  12. Add your palm oil to the egusi. Mix and set aside till your meat is soft
  13. Now scoop the mixture into the your meat as shown in the picture
  14. Cook till oil appears on the surface. Adjust seasoning to taste
  15. Now add your ugwu leaves. I washed it with salt and drain before adding
  16. Tadaa! It's ready. Typically igbo style. You should try it really.
  17. You can serve with any swallow of your choice.
  18. Yeppie! Enjoy
  19. Note: these egusi soup is healthier because the oil is cooked and not fried
